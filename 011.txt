
Question: Kiali pod is exposing port 20001 which is the GUI. How do you port forward to it?
          (Assuming name of kiali pod is saved in $KIALIPOD

Answer:

  echo _________________________________________________________________________________________
  echo
  echo starting pod forward 
  echo
  kubectl port-forward -n istio-system $KIALIPOD 20001:20001 &
  echo
  echo
  echo Now you can go to localhost:20001
  echo
